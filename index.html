Why It Matters

Biomedical micro-robotics and intraluminal device research often requires expensive prototyping, fragmented simulation workflows, and long experimental cycles.

MAM Nexus aims to reduce early-stage R&D friction by helping teams explore control logic, motion feasibility, and system behavior before committing to hardware.
